Exam overview

An Encyclopedia which not only gives you the latest information about Eligibility, Application Fee, Important Dates, Syllabus, Official Notifications etc. regarding your exams, but also provides you the most trusted Preparation Resources & Study Materials/ Books, along with the Previous Years Papers & Mock Test Papers.

What MakeMyPath Offers

make my practice

In this section, you can do unlimited practice in any chapter of any subject as per your exam’s syllabus. Also you can view the solution instantly after attempting the question.

make my Paper

In this you can generate test paper according to your preparation level. You have freedom to choose Subjects, Chapters, Numbers of Questions, Total Time and Difficulty Level. Also you will get detailed result analysis.

make my Path

It is a very unique AI based tool which will help you to track your preparation level and accordingly guide you to plan your studies as per chosen goal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why I should use this?
This platform provides the right tools for learning, AI enabled preparation path, and an unlimited set of question papers with detailed result analysis.
What is make my practice ?
In this section you can do unlimited practice in any subject and chapter as per your exam syllabus. Also you can view the solution instantly after attempting any question.
What is make my paper ?
In this you can generate test paper according to your preparation level. You have freedom to choose Subjects, Chapters, Numbers of Questions, Total Time and Difficulty Level. Also you will get detailed result analysis.
What is make my path ?
It is a very unique AI based tool which will help you to track your preparation level and accordingly guide you to plan your studies as per your chosen goal.

Yes, you can share test papers with friends and let them monitor your progress.
Can I use this for multiple exams from the same login?
Yes, you can use it for as many exams as you wish with the same login.
Is this application Free?
Yes, it is absolutely free with an unlimited set of questions.
Can it be also used by teachers or mentors?
Yes, teachers or mentors can create hassle-free test papers for students (with a solution.).
